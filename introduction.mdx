## What is Rotations?

Rotations is a modern music player SaaS application that reimagines how you experience music. Built from first principles, it combines curated playlists (called "rotations") with powerful listening graph visualization to give you deeper insights into your music habits.

## Core Philosophy

Rotations is built around a simple question: **What if you could have better control over your daily music rotation?**

Instead of algorithmic recommendations, Rotations gives you:

- **Hand-curated control** over what enters your rotation
- **Visual insights** into your listening patterns through graph visualization
- **Multi-source support** to aggregate music from your favorite platforms
- **Offline-first architecture** so your music is always available

## Key Features

### Current Rotation System

Listening to a song automatically adds it to your Current Rotation. You control your rotation's radius and get recommended songs outside your current bubble.

### Listening Graph Visualization

Every song you listen to becomes a node in a directed, weighted graph. Transitions between songs create edges, revealing patterns in your listening habits through:

- **PageRank analysis** - Discover your most influential songs
- **Cluster detection** - See natural groupings in your music taste
- **Path finding** - Explore how songs connect in your listening history

## Architecture Overview

Rotations consists of three main components:

1. **Site** (`site/`) - Astro-based music player frontend
2. **Graph Frontend** (`graph-frontend/`) - React-based listening graph visualization
3. **Graph Server** (`graph-server/`) - Node.js backend for graph analysis and data processing

<Info>
  The application uses TinyBase for local-first data storage with automatic localStorage persistence, ensuring your music library is always available.
</Info>
