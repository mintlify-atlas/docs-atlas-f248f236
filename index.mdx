## What is Rotations?

Rotations is a music player that reimagines how you interact with your music library. It's **import-only** — meaning you add music once and it's forever persisted. Every song you listen to automatically becomes part of your **Current Rotation**, giving you better control over your daily music experience.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Hand-Curated Rotations" icon="rotate">
    Your current rotation determines what you listen to. Control the radius and discover songs outside your rotation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Offline Mode" icon="download">
    All playlists and music are downloaded automatically the first time they stream.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Rotation Support" icon="layer-group">
    Create different rotations for different listening contexts and moods.
